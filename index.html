About me

Hello. I can only tell you about myself in brief. Consul Han needs my attention in solving a kidnapping case. His daughter has been kidnapped by Juntao. Poor Soo-Yung!

I spend my time in a few different locations:

  • Hong Kong, China, where I'm originally form.
  • Los Angeles, USA, where I am currently.
  • New York, USA, when I want to see the Knicks play.
  • Paris, France, for family reunions.

I'd be farther along in my investigation if I was working alone. Instead I'm working with this character from LAPD. He doesn't like the Beach Boys for some reason. I think they're great American Music.

Our trail so far has taken us to:

  1. A pool hall.
  2. The Chinese Consulate.
  3. An abandoned warehouse.
  4. Chinatown for some eel and camel hump.

Who knows, maybe this East meets West experience will work out after we upstage the FBI, shake down a pool hall, and get funky to some War. Perhaps a sequel or two will come out after this...
